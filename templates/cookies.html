1. Why We Use Cookies

Like many professional business platforms, TechKnow Solutions uses small data files termed cookies to augment navigation and ensure compliance. We utilize them to store your session identifiers, handle secure cookie authentication inside the administrative panel, retain preferred dark/light styles, and filter bot-driven crawler spikes.

2. Third-Party Cookie Disclosures (Google AdSense)

Google and its partners utilize DoubleClick cookies to serve targeted advertisements to users, monitoring behavior across various portals to improve ad relevancy. Disabling cookies will preserve basic site reading, but may alter custom settings and advertising accuracy.

3. Controlling Your Settings

You can reject or wipe cookies on your personal devices from your specific browser options. For details on wiping cookies or controlling preferences, please see your browser’s documentation or visit AllAboutCookies.org.
