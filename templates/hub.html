Frequently Asked Questions

Are these tools safe to use?

Yes. The tools run in the browser and are designed for learning and first-level checking. Avoid pasting private banking passwords, confidential emails or sensitive company data into any online website.

Can this replace professional cybersecurity testing?

No. These tools help with awareness and quick checks. For business systems, use proper security assessments, patch management, backups, access control and qualified technical review.

Who is this hub for?

Students, ICT officers, small businesses, CCNA learners, cybersecurity beginners and everyday users who want practical technology guidance.

Does the subnet calculator help with CCNA?

Yes. It gives network ID, broadcast address, subnet mask, host range and usable host count, which are common CCNA subnetting concepts.

Why did the CV scanner fail on my PDF?

Some PDFs are scanned images, not selectable text. For those files, paste the CV text into the text box or upload a text-based PDF/DOCX.

Why is the internet speed result an estimate?

The tester measures performance between your browser and this website server. Exact ISP benchmarking may differ depending on distance, routing, Wi‑Fi quality, device load and server conditions.
